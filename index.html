DR. JEFFREY BEAN, DPM, FACFAS

Diplomate, American Board of Foot and Ankle Surgery

Board Certified in Foot Surgery

Dr. Jeff has been practicing podiatry in his home town of Carson City since 2002. After a decade of practicing medicine and surgery with his father, and learning from Dr. Kim’s experience, Dr. Jeff is now practicing solo. He loves helping people live more comfortably and improving the quality of their lives. He enjoys his staff, who are like family.

He is married since 1994 and has 4 sons. He enjoys skiing, bicycle riding, and hiking with his family, among other outdoor activities while taking in the endless and diverse beauty of this area. His wife, Amalinda, and his daughter-in-law, Madison, work at the office. His youngest son and his granddaughter can often be seen in the office in the afternoon.
