SIERRA CENTER FOR FOOT SURGERY

OUR SURGERY CENTER

Sierra Center for Foot Surgery is a fully-accredited ambulatory foot and ankle surgery center attached to our Sierra Podiatry Center clinic. We are accredited by AAAASF, meaning our standards far exceed the state and federal requirements for surgical centers. Dr Jeffrey Bean performs most of the major surgeries that require IV sedation in our on-site surgery center. If you need foot or ankle surgery, you can take comfort in knowing that Dr. Jeffrey Bean is the only surgeon who is double Board Certified in Foot Surgery and Reconstructive Rearfoot/Ankle Surgery in our area. He has performed well over 5,000 surgeries and has extensive experience in a wide variety of surgical procedures for the foot and ankle. Dr. Bean completed a year-long internship, followed by a 3-year surgical residency at one of the premier foot and ankle residency programs in the country. He is a Diplomat of the American Board of Foot and Ankle Surgery, and was the Valedictorian of his podiatric medical school class, with multiple professional , academic and clinical awards.

Sierra Center for Foot Surgery exists to provide the utmost in convenience, comfort, and safety for our patients who require foot and ankle surgery. All of your pre- and post-operative appointments will be done in our clinic that is on-site. Most patients prefer an ambulatory surgical center over a hospital because of substantial cost savings, more convenient and relaxed environment, more specialized care, and a safer setting with lower complication and infection rates. Sierra Center for Foot Surgery offers a highly skilled surgical staff that is friendly specialized in foot and ankle surgery. You will be attended by at least one nurse at all times, who’s attention is focused only on you.
