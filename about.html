ABOUT SIERRA PODIATRY

OUR CLINIC

When you need personal, attentive care for your feet, turn to a podiatrist in Carson City who cares. At Sierra Podiatry Center, we strive to provide individualized treatment for each person, not just a “cookie cutter” approach. We specialize in the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of all problems of the foot and ankle. We want to help you get back on your feet and return to the activities you love.

From ingrown toenails to traumatic injuries; from minimally invasive procedures to complex reconstructive surgery, Sierra Podiatry Center and Sierra Center for Foot Surgery is equipped to handle all your foot and ankle needs. We are a family-owned business servicing Carson City and surrounding communities since 1973, the longest continually running podiatry, or foot and ankle, practice in Northern Nevada. We have a more personalized, family-based approach to healthcare. Our practice is designed to give each patient the care and attention they deserve, unlike the ever-more-common corporate high-volume mass production approach.

Dr. Jeffrey Bean is the preferred podiatrist in Carson City and the surrounding area. He has privileges at Carson Tahoe Regional Medical Center, Sierra Surgery Hospital, and Carson Tahoe Surgery Center, as well as our own ambulatory surgery center, Sierra Center for Foot Surgery. Many physicians refer their patients to Dr. Jeffrey Bean. Other podiatrists refer their patients with more difficult and complicated conditions to Dr. Bean. He is trusted by both his patients and his peers.
